Why We Built Walnut

Imagine being an international student in a fast-paced lecture where the professor speaks quickly in a language you haven't yet mastered. You have to translate constantly, take notes, and understand concepts simultaneously. By the time you process one sentence, the next is already gone. As UBC students, we experienced firsthand the challenges of language barriers in lectures, leading us to create Walnut - a solution that makes learning accessible to everyone.
